# OpenHRP - Employee Relations Management

## 📖 Introduction
OpenHRP is an open-source Human Resource Platform used to manage employee relations effectively.

---

## ⚖️ 1. Grievance Tracking
- Employees can raise complaints related to workplace issues.
- HR records and tracks grievances systematically.
- Ensures transparency and proper resolution.

Example:
Employee: Rahul  
Issue: Salary delay  
Status: Pending  

---

## ⚠️ 2. Disciplinary Actions
- HR records employee misconduct or policy violations.
- Actions include warnings, suspension, or termination.
- Maintains fairness and documentation.

Example:
Employee: Anu  
Action: Warning  
Reason: Late attendance  

---

## 🤝 3. HR as a Peacemaker
- HR acts as a mediator between employees and management.
- Resolves conflicts through communication.
- Ensures a healthy work environment.

Flow:
Employee → HR → Resolution  

---
